
## Section: Digital Well-being and Social Media Etiquette

### ✅ Do’s

> **"Your digital footprint is a reflection of your character in the virtual world."**

* **Think before posting:** Share content that reflects positively on you and won’t harm your reputation later.
* **Protect your privacy:** Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and review privacy settings regularly.
* **Be respectful:** Engage in constructive discussions and avoid offensive or discriminatory language.
* **Verify information:** Fact-check news or claims before reposting to avoid spreading misinformation.
* **Balance screen time:** Set boundaries to prevent social media from interfering with studies, sleep, or real-life interactions.

---



### ❌ Don’ts

> **"Digital mistakes can be permanent; caution is the best preventive measure."**

* **Don’t overshare personal details:** Avoid posting sensitive information like addresses, phone numbers, or financial data.
* **Don’t engage in cyberbullying:** Never use social media to harass, insult, or demean others.
* **Don’t post impulsively:** Avoid posting when angry, upset, or under the influence—it can lead to regret later.
* **Don’t plagiarize content:** Give credit when sharing others’ work, whether it’s text, images, or videos.
* **Don’t ignore digital footprints:** Remember that deleted posts may still be archived or screenshotted.
